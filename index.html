What is Breathwork ?

Breathwork is a New Age term, for various breathing practices in which the conscious control of breathing is said to influence a person's mental, emotional, or physical state, with a therapeutic effect.

Benefits Of Breathwork

Research on breathwork is promising. Potential health benefits of breathwork include:

Alkalizing your blood PH:

Breathwork techniques can help regulate the pH levels in your blood, creating a more alkaline environment, which is associated with better health and reduced risk of chronic diseases.

Anti-inflammatory effect:

Deep breathing exercises have been shown to reduce inflammation in the body by activating the parasympathetic nervous system and decreasing the production of stress hormones like cortisol.

Elevating your mood:

Conscious breathing practices can increase the release of endorphins and serotonin, neurotransmitters associated with feelings of happiness and well-being.
